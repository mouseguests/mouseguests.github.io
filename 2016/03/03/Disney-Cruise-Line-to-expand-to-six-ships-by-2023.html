Disney Cruise Line to expand to six ships by 2023

03 March 2016 on Disney Cruise Line, News

In Disney’s annual shareholder’s meeting this morning (and then quickly followed by the Disney Parks Blog), Bob Iger confirmed that two new ships will be added to the Disney Cruise Line fleet. The first will come in 2021 and the next in 2023.

Our first thought was "What will they be named?" So far, no names have been revealed…​but we’ll keep you posted.
